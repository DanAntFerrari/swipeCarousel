swipeCarousel

Super simple carousel

Slider with touch swipe and drag.

Option for:

  • autoslide (state/interval/loop)
  • arrow
  • index indicators dots/spot
  • item for row
  • callback
  • fade option
  • drag on off (TODO:remove hammer library request)
  • drag on off
